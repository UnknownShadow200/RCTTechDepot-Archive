Track Element Codes

Last Updated Oct 22, 2002

Some examples are discussed here:

60á indicates a track segment which is sloped upward at about 60 degrees.

L ¼ D5 bank indicates a track segment which is a banked left handed quarter turn with a diameter of 5 tiles.

R 1/8 bank OTD indicates a track segment which is a banked right handed one-eighth turn. It starts as a regular orthogonal element but ends as a diagnonal element. OTD = orthogonal to diagonal. DTO = diagonal to orthogonal.

Diag R bank to 25â indicates a diagonal (straight) element which starts as a right sided bank and ends as a downward slope of about 25 degrees.

The Water Coaster uses the codes for "covered" water segments (even though the segment are not covered on that coaster). Italicized items in braces {like this}are not used in tracks but are included for completeness. Items in blue are available only in RCT2.
